Figma Make Prompt — Onboarding Body Scan
Design the onboarding body scan screen for SONA 
mobile app. This screen comes after the user 
pairs their wearable device.

PURPOSE
The user uses their phone camera to scan their 
body so SONA can generate a personalized wireframe 
body model that matches their actual body type, 
gender, and proportions.

SCREEN FLOW — 3 steps

STEP 1 — INTRO SCREEN
- Simple centered layout on white background
- Headline: "Let's map your body"
- Subtext: "We'll use your camera to create a 
  personalized body model. This takes about 
  30 seconds."
- Small privacy note below: 
  "Your scan is processed on-device and 
  never stored or shared."
- Large primary button: "Start Scan"
- Skip option in small text below button

STEP 2 — CAMERA SCAN SCREEN
- Full screen camera view
- A human body outline overlay on the camera 
  as a guide — showing the user where to 
  position themselves
- Simple instruction text at top:
  "Stand 6 feet away and face the camera"
- Subtle animated guide around the body 
  outline that pulses when the user is 
  in the right position
- A progress indicator showing scan stages:
  Front view → Side view → Complete
- Calm, minimal UI — camera should be the 
  focus, not buttons
- When scan is complete: brief confirmation 
  animation

STEP 3 — RESULT SCREEN
- Shows the generated wireframe spiderweb 
  body model based on the scan
- Headline: "Your body map is ready"
- Subtext: "We've created a model based on 
  your body. You can update this anytime."
- Two options below the model:
  Primary button: "Looks good, continue"
  Secondary: "Retake scan"

DESIGN NOTES
- Keep white background throughout all steps
- Camera screen can have a dark overlay with 
  the camera feed underneath
- The body outline guide should feel 
  helpful not clinical
- Progress should feel fast and effortless 
  — this is a 30 second moment not a 
  medical procedure
- Tone: simple, reassuring, quick
